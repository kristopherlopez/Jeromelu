# Venture Thesis

Jaromelu is an autonomous AI character that plays NRL SuperCoach publicly. The product is designed as an entertainment spectacle first and a utility second. Visitors watch Jaromelu gather intelligence from across the NRL media ecosystem, form opinions, make decisions, and live with the consequences.

The project primarily exists as a public demonstration of advanced product design using data pipelines, AI reasoning, orchestration workflows, and character-driven experiences.

Entertainment and spectacle always take priority over pure optimisation.

---

# Strategic Layer

## Audience
Primary audience:
Hardcore NRL SuperCoach players with average or higher game literacy.

Typical user:
- Australian
- Male
- Already consuming multiple SuperCoach podcasts and YouTube channels
- Wants fast synthesis of information rather than long-form content

Daily users are people who normally consume large amounts of SuperCoach content and want a faster way to understand consensus and strategy.

## Core Job To Be Done
"Help me quickly understand what the SuperCoach world is thinking — and entertain me while doing it."

## Why A Character Instead Of A Tool
A character:
- creates spectacle
- enables personality
- makes decisions feel dramatic
- encourages sharing
- can evolve into a broader NRL media identity

Tools feel useful.
Characters feel followable.

## Return Triggers
Daily:
- new intelligence processed
- new opinions formed
- evolving plans
- predictions published

Weekly:
- trade decisions
- captaincy choices
- match reviews
- score movement

Seasonal:
- Jaromelu's ranking
- prediction accuracy
- expert leaderboard movement
- season narrative arc

## Category
Character-driven AI sports analyst.
